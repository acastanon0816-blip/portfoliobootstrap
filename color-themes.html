Five Color Themes

I created the themes below in a palette form in Photoshop. Each theme represents a design type that I could use in various rooms in my home, including the homeschool space for my daughter. I also share the mood each palette communicates.

Theme Name Scheme Type Best For Palette Image Explanation
Soothing Coastal Analogous (cool) Bathroom, Spa, Wellness, Health
#1B4965 #5FA8D3 #CAE9FF #62B6CB #3A86A8
Cool blues/greens create a calm, trustworthy mood and support readability.
Dynamic Contrast Complementary Modern Home Office, Ads
#003049 #d62828 #f77f00 #fcbf49 #EAE2B7
Strong opposites create energy and draw attention to key content.
Warm & Inviting Warm palette Community, family brands
#8e3b46 #d38c5a #f4a259 #f4ebd0 #c85c5c
Warm tones feel friendly and inviting while still allowing hierarchy.
Minimally Modern Neutral + accent Kitchens, Bathrooms, Professional portfolios
#111111 #444444 #888888 #f5f5f5 #2d6a4f
Neutrals keep the design clean; one accent color adds emphasis. For kitchens or bathrooms a metallic accent adds another texture.
Primary Fun Triadic Classroom, Play area, Kids brands, learning
#ff595e #ffca3a #8ac926 #1982c4 #6a4c93
Bright balanced colors create a fun tone while staying structured.
