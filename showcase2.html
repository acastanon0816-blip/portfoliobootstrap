Project Overview

The aim of this project was to create a brand package in Adobe Photoshop. The goal was to design a consistent visual identity and apply it across multiple marketing materials. The final package includes a logo, a business card design, and an Instagram cover graphic to demonstrate how the brand would appear across platforms.

Deliverables

1) Logo

I created a logo that represents the brand in a clear and audience appropriate way. I focused on readability, balance, and a design that works at different sizes.

2) Business Card

The business card uses the same brand elements (type, colors, spacing) to keep the identity consistent. I included a clean layout to make contact information easy to read.

3) Instagram Cover

This Instagram cover graphic was designed to match the brandâ€™s look and feel while being visually engaging for social media. I used strong hierarchy and simple shapes to help the text stand out.

Tools and Techniques Used

  • Layers and layer organization: kept each element editable and easy to update or copy.
  • Shape tools and alignment: created clean layouts and consistent spacing.
  • Type Tool: to keep text readable and balanced, but also fun
  • Transformation Tool: allowed resizing without losing quality.
  • Exporting assets: saved final files in web-friendly formats for portfolio display.

Challenges and Improvements

One challenge I faced was learning how to subtract shapes correctly when creating the banner. The option was not available because I was not using the correct tool settings. After lots of trial and error, at times deleting the layer and starting all over again, I finally adjusted the shape mode and was able to complete it properly.
